SWEET AND SOUR FISH FILLET

COOKING PROCEDURE



  • PROCEDURE
  1. FILLET THE FISH CUT INTO THIN STRIPS AND SEASON IT WITH SALT AND PEPPER.
  2. CREATE BATTER BY MIXING EGG, FLOUR, AND WATER. SEASON IT WITH SALT AND PEPPER.
  3. DIP THE FISH INTO THE BATTER THEN FRY FOR 10 MINS.
  4. STIR FRY VEGETABLES (BELL PEPPER, ONIONS) AND THE PINEAPPLE.
  5. CREATE SAUCE BY COMBINING KETCHUP, VINEGAR, AND SUGAR. THEN ADD TO THE STIR FRIED VEGETABLES.
  6. MIX THE FRIED FISH.

    7. AND YOUR DONE!!
